FAQ

A short explanation of how supply support works and where responsibility boundaries sit.

Is Fullhouse the equipment supplier?

No. Fullhouse supports the transaction on the customer side: finding suppliers, aligning terms, identifying risks and preparing the deal for signing. Any different role is agreed separately.

Can Fullhouse join only for negotiations?

Yes. We can work with an already selected supplier: strengthen the negotiating position, pursue commercial preferences, record agreements and check that material terms reach the documents.

What materials does the customer receive?

Depending on scope: supplier lists, comparison matrices, negotiation minutes, aligned technical and commercial terms, risk maps, security-measure recommendations and draft contract wording.
